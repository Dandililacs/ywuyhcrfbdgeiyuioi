Printalyst

Printalyst is aims to make 3D printing accessible for underprivileged or remote people.

Filler text

To be eligible, you have to explain efforts you took to find public access printers such as looking for local libraries, makerspaces, and fab labs.

We directly provide free 3D printing for underprivileged youth. We also provide 3D printers to public access spaces to help manage costs, especially internationally.
